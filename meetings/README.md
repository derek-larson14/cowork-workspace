# Meeting Notes

`/meeting` looks here when it answers questions about your meetings.

In Co-Work it checks the **Granola** and **Google Calendar** connectors first. This folder is the local fallback, and a place to keep notes from anywhere else.

**Ways to fill it:**

1. **Granola connector** - Enable Granola in Co-Work and `/meeting` reads your notes directly. Nothing to copy.
2. **Copy-paste** - Export notes from Otter, Notion, or wherever and drop them here as Markdown.
3. **Write your own** - New `.md` files, using `example-meeting.md` as a template.
